2020/2021 - LMS soliton lectures
  Institution: London Mathematical Society
  Details:

The LMS have funded a short set of lectures on topological solitons aimed at graduate students. These were delivered in collaboration with Chris Halcrow, Josh Cork and Calum Ross. They cover a number of topics including; introduction to solitons, domain walls, vortices and gauge theories, instantons and the Nahm transform, and nuclear Skyrmions.
The lectures can be found [here].
